# Digital Literacy Project Report

## Task 1: Presentation
In this task, a presentation was created to explain the concept of digital literacy and its importance in today’s world. Digital literacy refers to the ability to use digital tools and technologies effectively and responsibly. The presentation covered various aspects such as the use of the internet, online communication, and digital safety. It also highlighted how digital literacy is essential in education, professional work, and daily life. Examples were included to show how people use smartphones, computers, and online platforms for learning, communication, and transactions. The presentation emphasized the importance of being aware of online risks such as cyber threats and misinformation. Overall, this task helped in developing a clear understanding of digital literacy and improved presentation and research skills.

## Task 2: Portfolio Creation
In this task, a digital portfolio was created using different online platforms to build a professional online identity. Profiles were made on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. GitHub was used to upload and manage project files, LinkedIn was used to create a professional profile for networking, and Kaggle was explored for learning data science concepts. Screenshots of all profiles were taken and uploaded as proof of work. This task helped in understanding the importance of maintaining a digital presence and showcasing skills online. It also provided exposure to platforms commonly used in the industry. Creating these profiles improved confidence in using professional tools and prepared a foundation for future career opportunities.

---

## Task 3: Digital Platforms
This task involved exploring different digital platforms and understanding their practical applications. A Google Form was created to conduct a Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz with multiple-choice questions. Responses from the form were collected and automatically recorded in Google Sheets, which helped in understanding data collection and analysis. Additionally, basic Python problems were solved on HackerRank to develop problem-solving and coding skills. This task demonstrated how digital tools can be used for surveys, data handling, and skill development. It also improved familiarity with online platforms and enhanced technical and analytical abilities.

---

## Task 4: Email Etiquette & Social Media Guidelines
In this task, the focus was on improving digital communication skills. Two formal emails were written following proper email etiquette, including subject lines, greetings, and clear message structure. A checklist of social media do’s and don’ts was also created, highlighting safe and responsible behavior online. The importance of maintaining professionalism, respecting others, and avoiding inappropriate content was emphasized. A short write-up explained how poor digital communication can lead to misunderstandings and how proper etiquette improves clarity and professionalism. This task helped in understanding the importance of communication in the digital world and building a positive online presence.

---

## Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness
This task focused on understanding cybercrime and ways to prevent it. A case study on phishing attacks was prepared, explaining how attackers trick users into sharing sensitive information through fake emails or websites. The task also included a prevention checklist with safety tips such as using strong passwords, avoiding suspicious links, and enabling two-factor authentication. Special emphasis was given to UPI safety and not sharing PINs or approving unknown requests. Information about reporting cybercrime was included, such as the official portal and helpline number. A reflection was written to highlight the importance of awareness and safe online practices. This task improved understanding of online threats and encouraged responsible digital behavior.
